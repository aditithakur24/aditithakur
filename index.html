About Myself

Hello! I’m Aditi Thakur, a dedicated medical graduate from Lalitpur, Nepal, with an MBBS degree from Universal College of Medical Sciences and Teaching Hospital (UCMS), Bhairahawa, Nepal. I’ve successfully completed both Step 1 and Step 2 of the USMLE, scoring 248 in Step 2, and have gained invaluable experience through observerships at Irvine Medical in California and Trident Hospital in Charleston. My journey in medicine has also been shaped by extensive community service in Nepal, where I’ve had the privilege of contributing to healthcare initiatives. Additionally, I’ve participated in numerous medical conferences to further my knowledge and skills. I am passionate about continuing my medical career in the USA and contributing to patient care through a residency program.
